🏞️ Trekking in Coorg

✅ Popular Treks:

Tadiandamol Peak (1,748 m) – Highest in Coorg, about 15 km round trip. Suitable for moderate to experienced trekkers.

Brahmagiri Hills – Through the Brahmagiri Wildlife Sanctuary; scenic forest trek.

Pushpagiri (Kumara Parvatha) –A challenging trail, very popular among seasoned hikers.

Nishani Motte – Ideal for beginners, with forest trails and ridge walks.

📅 Best Time: October to February

⏳ Duration: 4–6 hours (day treks), or overnight camping treks.

💰 Cost:Free (except forest entry fees/guide: ₹500–₹1,500)

🌊 River Rafting in Barapole

One of the most thrilling white-water rafting experiences in South India.
Rapids are categorized from Grade II to Grade IV.
Located near the Brahmagiri Wildlife Sanctuary.

📅 Best Time: July to September (during monsoon)

⏳ Duration:2–3 hours

💰 Cost:₹1,000–₹1,200 per person

🎯 Difficulty: Moderate to High

🚙 Mandalpatti Jeep Safari

A bumpy, off-road ride to the Mandalpatti Viewpoint, known for breathtaking sunrises and misty mountains.
Private vehicles aren’t allowed beyond a point—jeep rides are the only option.

📅 Best Time: October to May

⏳ Duration: 2–3 hours

Hanging Bridge:A hanging bridge opposite the falls provides a vantage point for panoramic views and photography

💰 Cost: ₹1,500–₹2,000 per jeep (4–6 people)

🌲 Camping in Coorg

Experience night under the stars amidst coffee plantations, riverbanks, or forest clearings.
Many eco-resorts offer tents, bonfire, and barbecue facilities.

📅 Best Time: October to March

💰 Cost: ₹800–₹2,000 per person (with meals and tent)

🔦 Popular Areas:Kakkabe, Madikeri, Bettigeri

🎯 Ziplining

Ziplining is an adventure activity where you are harnessed and slide down a steel cable stretched between two points—usually across forests, valleys, or coffee plantations. It gives you a bird’s eye view while flying above the landscape.

Available at adventure parks and coffee estate camps.
Short-distance ziplines for beginners and family fun.

📅 Best Time:All year (avoid heavy rain)

💰 Cost: ₹300–₹600 per ride

🐘 Dubare Elephant Camp

Elephant Bathing: Assist mahouts in giving elephants a bath in the river.
Feeding Sessions: Participate in feeding elephants with ragi, jaggery, sugarcane, bananas, and coconuts.

⏰ TimingsDaily from 9:00 AM to 11:00 AM and 4:30 PM to 5:30 PM.

Day Visit Package: Available from 8:30 AM to 2:30 PM, which includes elephant interaction, nature walk, breakfast, and lunch.

💰 Entry Fees

Elephant Interaction:₹670 per person for Indian citizens.

Boating:₹50–₹100 per person, depending on the type of boat.

Day Visit Package:₹1,600 per person, inclusive of elephant interaction, nature walk, breakfast, and lunch.
