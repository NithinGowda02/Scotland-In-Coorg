About Us

Welcome to Scotland in Coorg, your trusted gateway to the enchanting hills, lush valleys, and untamed adventures of Coorg – often referred to as the “Scotland of India.” Our name pays homage to the breathtaking landscapes and misty mountains that draw a striking resemblance to the Scottish Highlands, right here in the heart of Karnataka. Founded by passionate travel enthusiasts, our mission is to offer travelers an authentic, hassle-free, and memorable experience of Coorg’s natural beauty and cultural richness. Whether you're seeking solitude in nature, adventure in the wild, or a taste of local hospitality, we bring it all under one roof. We specialize in a wide range of travel services including guided trekking, affordable car rentals, handpicked hotel bookings, and thrilling adventure activities. Every experience we offer is thoughtfully curated to suit different travel styles, budgets, and preferences. What sets us apart is our local expertise and a deep love for Coorg. We collaborate with trusted local guides, eco-conscious hotels, and certified adventure professionals to ensure your safety, comfort, and satisfaction at every step of your journey. From mist-covered trails and coffee plantations to hidden waterfalls and ancient temples, Coorg is a land waiting to be explored – and we are here to make that exploration seamless and unforgettable. Whether you’re planning a weekend getaway, a romantic escape, a family holiday, or an adrenaline-pumping trip with friends, Scotland in Coorg promises to make your travel dreams come alive. Let us be your compass in Coorg — guiding you through the unexplored, the unexpected, and the unforgettable. Your adventure begins here.
